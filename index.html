How to Use Global Offers & Events

  • To add a new entry, click the green (+) button at the bottom-right corner.
  • Fill in details like Title, Business Name, Location, Description, Dates, and Type.
  • Click Upload to save. Your entry will show based on date validity.
  • Use search filters above to locate entries by business, country, or region.
  • Turn on Global View to show all entries regardless of location.
  • Use "H" button to view your full history of all entries.
  • You can Edit or Delete any entry from the displayed card.
  • Entries are stored locally in your browser using localStorage.
  • Future updates: image/video upload coming soon.
